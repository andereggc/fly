Financial Literacy For The Youth (FLY)

Empowering the youth to take control of their personal finances

About Us

Our Mission Is To Increase Financial Literacy And Fiscal Wellness By Empowering Young People To Understand And Take Control Of Their Personal Finances With The Intention That Those We Serve Will, In Turn, Utilize Their Knowledge And Skills Within Their Communities With The End Goal Of Providing A Springboard To Help Those Communities Thrive Economically. Through A Range Of Delivery Methods That Include Group Lessons, Community Forums, And Guest Speaker Sponsorships, We Provide Personal Financial Wellness Education On Budgeting, Taxes, Insurance, Loans, Credit And Debit, Financial Accounts, Stocks, Bonds, And Mutual Funds.

Member Benefits

Becoming A Member Will Give You Access To The Financial Literacy Education That Many Of Us Missed Out On From Traditional Schooling. In Becoming A Member, You Will Also Have The Option Of Going To Organizations In The Local Community To Teach Financial Wellness. Members Will Gain Exposure To Topics Such As Personal Finance & Financial Statements, Managing Income Taxes, Building And Maintaining Good Credit, Investment Fundamentals, Investing Through Mutual Funds, And Managing Risk.
